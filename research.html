Land Use and Land Cover Change Analysis

June 2024 Kannoth Padam, Kerala Field Research Team

Conducted field surveys and GIS analysis to study land use changes in the region over the past decade. The study focused on the impact of urbanization on agricultural lands and natural habitats.

Hydrological Impact Assessment

March 2024 Periyar River Basin Research Team

Studied the hydrological impacts of climate change on the Periyar River Basin, focusing on water resource management and flood risk assessment. Analyzed rainfall patterns, river discharge data, and modeled potential flood scenarios to assess impacts on local communities and agriculture.

Hydrology Climate Modeling Water Resources GIS Analysis
